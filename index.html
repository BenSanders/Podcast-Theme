About Podcast Theme

Podcast Theme is a free, open-source, Jekyll theme centered on podcasting. If you want to download and use this theme you may do so for free and even contribute to the GitHub as you see fit. I was even thinking about making this a WordPress theme at some point.

My hope with making this theme freely available is to just give people a nice start to putting up a podcast site. I also figured that it would give me something to show off to potential clients.
